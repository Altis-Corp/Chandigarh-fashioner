Our Story

About Chandigarh Fashioners

Chandigarh Fashioners is a trusted local fashion boutique in Pinjore offering elegant women's clothing for everyday wear, festive occasions, and special moments. Known for quality fabrics, stylish designs, and friendly service, the store has become a favorite shopping destination for women looking for affordable elegance.

Women-owned and community-loved, we believe every woman deserves to feel beautiful. Our carefully created collections blend traditional Indian aesthetics with contemporary fashion sensibilities.
